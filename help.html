Instructions

The objective of this game is for you the player to control Donald Trump navigate him to the presidency. The first part of the game is the Republican primary where Donald needs to collect delegates from states in the form of flags. If Donald can get to 1237, he wins the Republican nomination and gets the next part of the game, chase for the White House. The chase for the White House will involve Donald needing to collect electoral college votes from the same states. If Donald can get to 270, you win the game!

There's a catch though. You start with 4 health points. There's a cannon in the middle with an enemy wielding it. The enemy is dependent on your current score and progression - Marco Rubio, Ted Cruz, and Hillary Clinton. The cannon will rotate continuously and fire bullets. If you(Trump) gets hit by a bullet, you will lose a health point. Lose all health points and you lose the game.

There are additional enemies as well. They will appear as you progress through the game and follow him. If they get to Donald, Donald will lose one health point. These enemies are the assassin sent by the Establishment and the cartel member

They are also characters that have special abilities that appear during the game. One such character is Ben Carson. If you catch to Ben Carson, he will heal you and you will get one health point back. Another character is Ivanka Trump. When Ivanka is active, Donald will get 10% more in states with regards to electors and delegates.
