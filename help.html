How to Play

The Objective

The objective of this game is for you, the player, to control Donald Trump and to navigate him to the presidency. The first part of the game is the Republican primary where Donald needs to collect delegates (in the form of flags). If Donald can get to 1237 delegates, he wins the Republican nomination and advances to the next part of the game: The chase for the White House. The chase for the White House will involve Donald collecting electoral college votes from the same states. If Donald can get to 270, you win the game!

The Controls

Move Donald with the arrow keys


About the Game

You start with 4 health points. There's a cannon in the middle with an enemy wielding it. The enemy is dependent on your current score and progression - Marco Rubio, Ted Cruz, and Hillary Clinton. The cannon will rotate continuously and fire bullets. If Trump gets hit by a bullet, you will lose a health point. If you lose all health points then you lose the game.

The Good

Ben Carson
 If you catch up to Ben Carson, he will heal you and you will get one health point back.

Ivanka Trump
 When Ivanka is active, Donald will receive a 10% boost in states with regards to electors and delegates.

The Evil
The assassin chases Donald around the map, trying to decrease his health by one health point. Their speed and quantity increase as Donald progresses.

Assassin
The cartel chases Donald around the map, trying to decrease his health by one health point. Their speed and quantity increases as Donald progresses.

The Neutral

Jeb Bush
 When Jeb is active, the assassin and cartel's speeds are decreased.

Reporter
 When the reporter is activated, she randomly gives you either a good review, increasing the number of delegates and electors you receive for each state, or a bad review, decreasing the number of delegates and electors you receive for each state.
